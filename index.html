Welcome to Cyblytics

Cyblytics is an academic research lab at FAST NUCES Islamabad focused on AI, Cyber-Physical Systems, and future-oriented computer science innovation. Our mission is to explore tomorrow’s ideas, today.

Vision

To shape Pakistan’s digital future by driving world-class research, cultivating scientific talent, and making tech innovation accessible to all.

Aim

We aim to empower students and researchers to lead technological progress and push the boundaries of what's possible in computing.

Goals

  • Advance interdisciplinary research in CS, AI, IoT, and embedded systems.
  • Foster national and global collaborations for tech breakthroughs.
  • Support open-access science, reproducibility, and ethical tech development.
  • Encourage student innovation and real-world problem solving.
